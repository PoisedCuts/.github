# PoisedCuts

**Lossless video cuts for Mac. No subscription, no cloud, own forever.**

PoisedCuts is a native macOS app that lets you cut sections out of any video - including middles - and export a clean file without re-encoding. Open a recording, mark what you don't want, export. Done.

$29, once, forever.

---

## Features

- **Lossless by default** - passthrough export means zero quality loss, zero re-encoding wait
- **Native Mac app** - Swift/SwiftUI, not Electron. Launches instantly, respects your system
- **Keyboard-first** - I to mark in, O to mark out, Space to play, Cmd+E to export
- **Silence detection** - one click auto-finds and marks quiet sections
- **GIF export** - animated GIF output for quick sharing
- **Preview mode** - watch the final cut before exporting
- **Audio waveform** - see speech and silence at a glance on the timeline
